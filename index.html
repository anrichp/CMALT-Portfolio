CMALT Portfolio Structure

Contextual Statement

  • Brief professional biography
  • Current role and responsibilities
  • Career history in learning technology
  • Motivation for pursuing CMALT
  • Future career aspirations
Example

"As the eLearning Coordinator at XYZ University for the past five years, I've led the implementation of blended learning strategies across our undergraduate programmes. My journey in learning technology began..."

Core Area 1: Operational Issues

1a: Understanding of constraints and benefits of different technologies

Example: Comparing different Virtual Learning Environments for institutional adoption

1b: Technical knowledge and ability in learning technology use

Example: Developing and managing a Moodle-based course delivery system

1c: Supporting deployment of learning technologies

Example: Training academic staff on using video conferencing tools for remote teaching

Core Area 2: Teaching, Learning and Assessment Processes

2a: Understanding of teaching, learning and/or assessment processes

Example: Designing a flipped classroom model for a statistics module

2b: Understanding of your target learners

Example: Conducting needs analysis for adult learners in an online professional development programme

Core Area 3: Wider Context

3a: Understanding and engaging with legislation

Example: Ensuring GDPR compliance in student data collection for learning analytics

3b: Understanding and engaging with policies and standards

Example: Implementing accessibility standards in online course materials

Core Area 4: Communication and Working with Others

  • Example: Leading a cross-departmental team to develop a new digital literacy framework
  • Example: Presenting at conferences on innovative uses of AR in education

Specialist Area

Choose one or more areas of expertise, such as:

  • Producing learning materials/content
  • Project management in learning technology
  • Research in educational technology
  • Supporting and tutoring online learners
  • Designing tools and systems
Example

"My specialist area is in designing and implementing augmented reality (AR) experiences for science education. This includes..."

Future Plans

  • Short-term and long-term professional development goals
  • Areas for further skill development
  • Future projects or initiatives you aim to undertake

"In the next year, I plan to enhance my skills in learning analytics to better inform our course design decisions. Long-term, I aim to..."

Remember

  • Aim for ~500-600 words per subsection for the narrative parts
  • Provide succinct, relevant evidence (e.g., screenshots, certificates, project reports)
